## 🛍️ AI Shopping Assistant

Finding the right product shouldn’t feel like scrolling through endless noise… and honestly, that’s the problem this tries to solve. Most people don’t search with perfect keywords—they think in intent (“good running shoes under 5000”), and this assistant is built around that reality.

At its core, this app understands what you actually mean. It parses your input to detect **category, use-case, and budget**, then filters and ranks products accordingly. Instead of dumping options, it gives you a **short, relevant list**—things you can realistically choose from.

### What it does

- 🧠 **Intent Understanding**  
  Figures out what you're looking for (not just what you typed)  
  → e.g. “running shoes under 5000” → category + purpose + budget  

- 🔍 **Smart Filtering**  
  Removes irrelevant products based on category and constraints  

- 📊 **Ranking Logic**  
  Prioritises items based on:
  - relevance to intent  
  - price fit  
  - contextual tags (e.g. lightweight, durable)  

- ⚡ **Quick Recommendations**  
  Surfaces top 3 options—so you can decide faster, not overthink  

- 💬 **Simple Interaction (CLI-based)**  
  Clean input → clear, structured output  

---

### Why this matters

Most shopping experiences optimise for *exploration*.  
This is built for **decision-making**.

It’s a small step toward reducing:
- choice overload  
- time to decision  
- irrelevant browsing
